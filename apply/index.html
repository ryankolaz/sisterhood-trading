Three years ago, I was carrying golf bags for tips at a course in Florida. I was working outside in all weather, completely dependent on other people's generosity, and I knew with everything in me that this was not going to be my life forever.

The losses were hard. But what made it harder was the space. Almost every community I entered was male-dominated. I asked questions and got mocked. I showed up wanting to learn and left feeling like I didn't belong. There were nights I closed my laptop and sat in silence, genuinely believing I wasn't cut out for this.

When I started sharing it online, the DMs came flooding in. Hundreds of women, all saying the same thing: "I tried to get into trading, but no one made me feel welcome." "I thought I was the only one who felt this way."

I looked for a women-only trading community to send them to. It didn't exist. So I built one.
